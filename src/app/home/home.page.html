10 Verses 6 Chapters Progressive Reveal
1. Padapāṭha पदपाठ — Word-by-Word Each word recited separately, slowly and clearly. Perfect for beginners to learn exact pronunciation.
2. Vākyapāṭha वाक्यपाठ — Continuous Flow Natural flow recitation without breaks. Regular chanting style for rhythm and devotional practice.
3. Anuloma Pāṭha अनुलोमपाठ — Forward Repetition Line-by-line forward repetition. Build verses gradually by adding one line at a time.
4. Pratiloma Pāṭha प्रतिलोमपाठ — Reverse Recitation Reverse order chanting of lines or words. Strengthens memory and prevents forgetting.
5. Krama Pāṭha क्रमपाठ — Sequential Pairing Words recited in pairs: (word1+word2), (word2+word3). Powerful for precise retention.
6. Jatā Pāṭha जटापाठ — Braided Recitation Complex braid-like pattern: word1-word2, word2-word1, word1-word2-word3. Very secure method.
7. Ghana Pāṭha घनपाठ — Dense Recitation Most advanced technique. Multiple word combinations with zero chance of corruption.
8. Ucchāraṇa उच्चारण — Loud Pronunciation Loud chanting to align ears, tongue, and mind. Builds strong sound memory.
9. Likitha Pāṭha लिखितपाठ — Writing Practice Writing verses repeatedly while reciting. Strengthens visual and muscular memory.
10. Smaraṇa स्मरण — Silent Recollection Mental recitation without voice. Internalize verses and meditate on their meaning.
